Simon Says - Digital Version :
Test your memory and reflexes with this classic game in a digital format! Press any key or block to start the game. Watch closely as the game highlights a sequence of blocks. Your task is to repeat the pattern by pressing the blocks in the correct order. With each level, the sequence gets longer and more challenging. Stay focused, remember the pattern, and press the blocks in the right sequence from the very beginning to move to the next level.
